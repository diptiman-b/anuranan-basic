Our Mission

Anuranan is a grassroots initiative committed to uplifting children and women from marginalized communities by fostering empowerment, education, and engagement. Our goal is to help individuals discover their voice and agency in a society that must be built on the pillars of respect, dignity, and equality—irrespective of gender, caste, or class.

Scope of Work

Anuranan’s work extends across a wide spectrum of humanitarian efforts, including:
👩‍👧 Supporting children and women from underprivileged backgrounds through education and skill-building
🩸 Facilitating blood donation drives and medical support for children affected by thalassemia
🏡 Providing care and resources to children in orphanages and those whose mothers are incarcerated
🏥 Reaching out to rural communities to assist individuals in need with basic necessities and healthcare
🤝 Creating community-based development programs that enable long-term self-reliance and dignity

Our Vision

To be a guiding light for those struggling to meet their most fundamental needs, helping them build a better life through collective action and community support.

Our Team

We are a group of passionate individuals committed to social change.
