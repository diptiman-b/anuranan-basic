Anuranan Participates in Unai Netaji Seva Pratisthan’s Foundation Day Celebration – 15th August 2025

On Independence Day, 15th August 2025, Anuranan was honored to join the foundation day celebration of Unai Netaji Seva Pratisthan in Bongaon, North 24 Parganas, marking our second consecutive year of participation.

During the event, Mr. Binay Kumar Sinha and Mr. Mihir Kumar Das, representing Anuranan, presented a 3.5-litre Milton flask to the editor of Unai. We also contributed the fifth volume of "Ja Ichchhe Tai" to the Unai library, enriching their literary collection. Continuing last year’s tradition, the organization purchased 40 copies of the first volume of "Ja Ichchhe Tai" to gift meritorious children in the local community.

Mr. Binay Kumar Sinha delivered an engaging slideshow presentation on:

  • Rooftop gardening techniques
  • Orchid cultivation
  • Identifying poisonous plants in our surroundings
  • Indoor plants that may pose health risks

The program concluded with an interactive quiz competition, conducted by Mr. Binod Sahu through a slideshow, which was enthusiastically received by the students. The event saw active participation from Unai’s members, students from various schools, and community members — all of whom engaged deeply and enjoyed the day's proceedings.
